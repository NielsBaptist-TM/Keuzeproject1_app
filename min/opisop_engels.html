What does 'Op Is Op' do?


Weekly food distributions located at the St. Apollonialaan 198 in Mol. We're located behind the restaurant Apollon. Opening hours starting saturday at 8.30 until 12 u.

Every week we offer a wide collection of fresh vegetables and fruit. Of course, we believe it is very important that everybody is given an equal chance to aquire food here. A lot of families have troubles doing this, and that's something we hope to change.

Location

You can find us on saturday mornings between 8:30 and 12:00 on the given location below.
