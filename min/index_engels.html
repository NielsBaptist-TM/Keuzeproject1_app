Welkom at MIN

Actie MIN helps people in poverty in the Mol, Balen, Dessel and Reties regions, by providing them with basic needs such as food. We work with an entirely voluntary team, who really want to help people in need.

We try to help everybody that we can by listening to them, showing them respect and by helping them along the way, regardless of his or her beliefs and culture.

If you are in need of help, you can look here for more information on the store we have called "op is op". Or you can look here for some of the activities that we organize throughout the year.

If you wish to help people in need and want to contribute to our cause, you can do that here. We greatly appreciate all sponsoring or funding, because these recources give us the possibility to help more people.
